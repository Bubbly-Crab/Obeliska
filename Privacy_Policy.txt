Obeliska 开源社区隐私政策

生效日期：2024年6月1日

感谢您访问 Obeliska 开源社区（以下简称“本社区”）。我们重视您的隐私权，并致力于保护您的个人信息安全。本隐私政策旨在说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息，以及您在使用本社区服务时享有的权利。

1. 适用范围
本隐私政策适用于所有通过 https://obeliska.org（或其子域名）访问 Obeliska 开源社区的用户，包括但不限于浏览者、注册用户、贡献者、开发者和项目维护者。

2. 我们收集的信息
我们可能收集以下类型的个人信息：
- 账户信息：当您注册账户时，我们会收集您的用户名、电子邮件地址、头像（如有）等。
- 使用数据：包括您的IP地址、浏览器类型、访问时间、页面浏览记录、操作日志等。
- 内容贡献：您在社区中发布的代码、评论、文档、议题（Issues）、拉取请求（Pull Requests）等公开内容。
- 第三方登录信息：若您通过 GitHub、GitLab 等第三方平台登录，我们将获取其提供的公开用户信息（如用户名、邮箱、公开资料链接）。
- Cookie 和追踪技术：我们使用 Cookie 和类似技术来优化用户体验、分析流量和识别用户身份。

3. 信息使用目的
我们仅在以下情况下使用您的个人信息：
- 提供和维护社区服务；
- 验证用户身份，防止滥用和垃圾信息；
- 改进网站功能和用户体验；
- 发送与账户相关的通知（如安全提醒、系统更新）；
- 遵守法律法规及响应法律请求。

4. 信息共享与披露
我们不会将您的个人信息出售、出租或交易给第三方。但在以下情况下，我们可能披露信息：
- 获得您的明确同意；
- 为遵守法律法规、法院命令或政府要求；
- 为保护本社区、用户或公众的合法权益；
- 在社区合并、收购或资产转让中，相关信息可能作为转让的一部分被披露。

5. 数据存储与安全
您的个人信息将存储在安全的服务器上，主要位于中国境内。我们采取合理的技术和管理措施（如加密、访问控制）防止未经授权的访问、泄露、篡改或销毁。

6. 您的权利
根据相关法律法规，您享有以下权利：
- 访问、更正或删除您的个人信息；
- 撤回同意（如适用）；
- 导出您的数据（数据可携权）；
- 抗议或限制数据处理；
- 注销账户。

您可通过账户设置或联系隐私团队行使上述权利。

7. Cookie 政策
我们使用 Cookie 来识别用户、保存登录状态和分析网站流量。您可通过浏览器设置管理或禁用 Cookie，但部分功能可能受限。

8. 儿童隐私
本社区不面向13周岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。如发现此类情况，我们将立即删除相关数据。

9. 隐私政策更新
我们可能不时更新本政策。更新后的内容将在本页面发布，并标注新的生效日期。重大变更将通过公告或邮件通知用户。

10. 联系我们
如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问、投诉或请求，请通过以下方式联系我们：
邮箱：privacy@obeliska.org
官方网站：https://obeliska.org/privacy

Obeliska Open Source Community Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2024

Thank you for visiting the Obeliska Open Source Community (hereinafter referred to as "the Community"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, as well as the rights you have when using our services.

1. Scope of Application
This Privacy Policy applies to all users accessing the Obeliska Open Source Community via https://obeliska.org (or its subdomains), including but not limited to visitors, registered users, contributors, developers, and project maintainers.

2. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of personal information:
- Account Information: When you register an account, we collect your username, email address, avatar (if any), etc.
- Usage Data: Including your IP address, browser type, access times, page views, operation logs, etc.
- Content Contributions: Public content you post in the community, such as code, comments, documentation, issues, pull requests, etc.
- Third-party Login Information: If you log in via platforms like GitHub or GitLab, we receive publicly available user information (e.g., username, email, public profile link).
- Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to optimize user experience, analyze traffic, and identify users.

3. Purposes of Information Use
We use your personal information only for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain community services;
- To verify user identity and prevent abuse or spam;
- To improve website functionality and user experience;
- To send account-related notifications (e.g., security alerts, system updates);
- To comply with legal obligations or respond to legal requests.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose information under the following circumstances:
- With your explicit consent;
- To comply with laws, regulations, court orders, or government requests;
- To protect the legitimate rights and interests of the Community, users, or the public;
- In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, where such information may be disclosed as part of the transaction.

5. Data Storage and Security
Your personal information is stored on secure servers, primarily located within China. We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights
Under applicable laws and regulations, you have the following rights:
- To access, correct, or delete your personal information;
- To withdraw consent (where applicable);
- To export your data (right to data portability);
- To object to or restrict data processing;
- To deactivate your account.

You may exercise these rights through your account settings or by contacting our privacy team.

7. Cookie Policy
We use cookies to identify users, maintain login sessions, and analyze website traffic. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be limited.

8. Children's Privacy
The Community is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is discovered, we will promptly delete it.

9. Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Material changes will be communicated via announcements or email.

10. Contact Us
If you have any questions, complaints, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: privacy@obeliska.org
Website: https://bubbly-crab.github.io/Obeliska
